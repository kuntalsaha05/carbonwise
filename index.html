Climate Education Center

What is CO2e?

CO2e (Carbon Dioxide Equivalent) aggregates emissions from multiple greenhouse gases (like methane and nitrous oxide) based on their global-warming potential, using CO2 as the baseline unit.

Carbon Footprint Targets

To limit global warming to 1.5°C under the Paris Agreement, the average personal carbon footprint globally needs to drop under 2.0 tonnes per year by 2050 (down from ~16 tonnes in developed areas).

Offset vs Reduction

Direct emission reduction (riding trains instead of cars, lowering heating) prevents heating gases from reaching the atmosphere. Offset initiatives (planting trees) take decades to absorb equivalent amounts.
